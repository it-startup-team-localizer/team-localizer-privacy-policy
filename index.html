Privacy Policy for Team Localizer

Effective Date: 24.10.2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Team Localizer! We take your privacy and data security seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, especially when it comes to location data. By using our mobile application, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information: Team Localizer do not require any personal information. Your only identifier is username and id assigned by our system. Username do not need to be related to yor real data - it is just a way how you will be seen by other users on our platform.

2.2. Location Information: Team Localizer collects and stores location data from your device, including GPS coordinates. This information is used to provide location-based services and visualize your position on a map.

2.3. Chat Feature: Team Localizer allows you to communicate with other users through a chat feature. We collect all message send this way. It is not recommended to share personal, payment or contact information through chat.

2.4. Photos: Team Localizer let you make a profile picture and add pictures to places/events from your camera. All data you share this way will be shared with other users of Team Localizer.

3. Use of Location Information

3.1. Map Visualization: We use your location data to display your position on a map to provide you with the intended functionality of the app.

4. Sharing Location Information

4.1. With Other Users: Your location information is shared with other users of Team Localizer to facilitate map visualization and enable location-based interactions.

4.2. Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services for map visualization and location services, and they may have access to your location data. We will not sell, rent, or trade your location data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to safeguard your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Team Localizer is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
